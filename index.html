ELIZABETH EBUN DYNAMIC
WIDOWS EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION

Elizabeth Ebun Dynamic Widows Empowerment Foundation is a non-governmental and humanitarian body which was founded by Ms. Ogunnubi Titilayo with sole aim to provide humanitarian support and empower helpless widows aged between 25 and 45years.

Our major mandate is to help young widows to feed, educate, access appropriate medical services and able to give their children standard lives. The foundation focuses on ensuring helpless widows, old widow inclusive, are supported in all areas of human endeavors.

Losing one’s husband at ay point in time (whether as young or old woman) is never a thing of envy in Nigeria. The challenge associated with widowhood in Nigeria ranges from poverty and economic deprivation to marginalization and inhuman treatment in husband’s family, in churches as well in society which are made possible by customs and traditions.
