Who we are? Read our Story

Elizabeth Ebun Dynamic Widows Empowerment Foundation is a non-governmental and humanitarian body which was founded by Ms. Ogunnubi Titilayo with sole aim to provide humanitarian support and empower helpless widows aged between 25 and 45years.

Our major mandate is to help young widows to feed, educate, access appropriate medical services and able to give their children standard lives.

The foundation focuses on ensuring helpless widows, old widow inclusive, are supported in all areas of human endeavors. Losing one’s husband at ay point in time (whether as young or old woman) is never a thing of envy in Nigeria. The challenge associated with widowhood in Nigeria ranges from poverty and economic deprivation to marginalization and inhuman treatment in husband’s family, in churches as well in society which are made possible by customs and traditions.

Today Statistics showed that we have over eight million widows and with estimate twenty-one million children in Nigeria. These widows are living in poverty, many have been abused, ostracized and abandoned. Unknown number of them have been targeted for rape, subjected to emotional and physical torture, prostitution, forced re-remarriage etc. This trend is worrisome. Because of its multiplier effect on the widows and their children as well as the society at large.

What is more shocking is the low level of awareness of this apparent gross violation of their rights and dignity by well-to-do individuals in the society. Furthermore, the graveside promises to the deceased children and widows are most of the time left unfulfilled.

Therefore Elizabeth Dynamic Widows foundation shall strictly adhere to the biblical command in the book of Isaiah chapter 1:7 which say “ learn to do good, seek justice, relive the oppressed, judge the fatherless, plead for the widows) in her efforts to providing succor to the widows, their children and less privileged in Nigeria.
